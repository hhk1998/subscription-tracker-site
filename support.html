FAQ

Do I need an account?

No. Subscription Tracker works without an account and stores subscription data locally.

Will reminders work offline?

Yes. Payment reminders use iOS local notifications scheduled on your device.

Where is my subscription data stored?

Your subscription data is stored locally on your device. Widgets use local App Group data.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open Settings in the app, then tap Restore Purchase.

What should I include in a support email?

Please include your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.
