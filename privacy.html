Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Local storage

Subscription Tracker stores your subscription information locally on your device. The app does not require an account to add, edit, delete, or view subscriptions.

No sale of subscription data

We do not sell your subscription data. We do not use your subscription list for tracking, advertising, analytics, or profiling.

Notifications

Local notifications are used only for payment reminders you choose to enable. Reminder scheduling happens on your device through iOS local notifications.

Widgets

Widgets use local data shared through the iOS App Group container so they can show upcoming payments. Widget data remains local to your device.

In-app purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. Subscription Tracker uses Apple StoreKit to unlock eligible premium features after a verified purchase.

No tracking technologies

This website and the app do not use analytics, external scripts, cookies, or tracking pixels.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support at h.h0101@yahoo.com.
